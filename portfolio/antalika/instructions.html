How Scandy works!

Do you think that it is impossible to buy the latest model of iPhone for just 45 $? Or the long-awaited new tablet for 95 $ ?

Scandy is a unique and urgent online auction, which allows to become the owner of a latest model of a smartphone, audio and video technologies, laptops and other things with discounts up to 90%. We offer only original products at our auction. The products have guarantees and quality certificates.

Sing up and get 20 bonus bids!

Why is Scandy better than a store?

1. We offer you the opportunity to buy goods at a great discount. On average, the discount can be up to 90%. The winner pays only the cost of the goods on which the auction finished. The delivery is free for the winner.

2. If you did not win the auction, you have the opportunity to purchase this product at a market price. The cost of bids you spent at this auction will be deducted from the value of the goods. In other words, you don’t lose anything.So instead of going to the store to buy a new phone or a laptop, take part in auctions on Scandy and save up to 90% of the value of the goods.

3. Scandy exhibits only the most popular examples of home appliances and electronics, the latest development of brand computers and smartphones. We try to choose only the best for the participants of our fantastic auctions.

4. The rules of the auction are very simple. You buy a certain bids package, place them on the desired item to try to win the necessary product at the lowest price. The minimum price of the bids package is 100 $, 10 $ per each bid. The person who placed the last bid is the winner!

The principle of the auction Scandy is very simple!

Step 1: Sign up and get 20 bonus bids to your account. Also, to start playing, you have to buy bids. The minimum price of the bids package is 100 $, the cost of a single bid is 10 $.

Step 2. Choose the product and participate in the auction. Scandy experts watch over the novelties on the market. You can be sure: you will find what you’ve been dreaming of.

Step 3: Place your bids. The starting price of the goods is 100 $. Each bid increases the price of goods for 10 . The auction lasts from 10 to 30 seconds. Each bid resets the timer and the auction starts over. The participant whose bid was the last, if was not outbargained by somebody else, is considered to be the winner.

Step 4: You can pick up the goods you won at the auction by paying the cost on which the corresponding auction finished. To do this, use the button "Shopping carts" at the auction or in a personal account "My wins." After the payment is received, we will send the goods to the indicated address. The delivery for the winner is FREE throughout the Russian Federation.

Step 5. If you did not win, you can still buy the product at the market price. The cost of bids you spent at this auction will be deducted from the value of the goods. To use this feature, click on the cart icon in the auction window and make anorder.
