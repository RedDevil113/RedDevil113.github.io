About the project

The project Auction.xxx provides you with an exclusive opportunity to purchase completely new and most popular things at an incredibly low price.

Our auction functions according to the Scandinavian system of bidding. Auction.xxx is an auction in which the goods or service is offered for sale at minimal cost. Bidders make bets with fixed pitch. For each bid the participant will be charged a fee. The duration of bidding is set in the beginning, however, after each bid it increases by a predetermined amount. The winner is the participant who placed the last bid. The starting price of lots for all goods is only 10-40% of the market value, in Belarusian rubles. After each bid the lot price increases by a fixed pitch of 50 rubles, and the time before the end varies depending on the number of bids placed by bidders. The winner of the auction is considered to be the participant who placed the last bid before the end of the bidding. As a result, the winner of the bidding gets an expensive thing at a ridiculous price. The foreground task of the project Auction.xxx is honesty and openness to our participants. We work legally and in full accordance with the

Belarusian legislation. Besides that, our experts have introduced protection against robotic programs or as they are called by people "bots". We will be happy to provide you with further information about this project and answer your questions on the forum of our auction. We care about you and therefore, in case you win, you won’t have to immediately pay the auction price of the goods and go somewhere to pick it up. You can always ask to ship the goods you won to any place in Belarus and pick it up in the nearest post office.

The payment of the auction value of the goods is made only upon the receipt of the winning. The delivery takes from 3 to 15 days. We make every effort to provide our customers with the best possible conditions for participation in the auction Auction.xxx. The goods sold at auction Auction.xxx, are completely new and are delivered to the winner in their original packaging, with the guarantee and all the necessary documents. The project Auction.xxx is not a gamble, lottery or contest.

Can I cancel the bid?

No,that's impossible.

Can I spend more than the value of the goods?

In most auctions the lot has a buyout price. This is the market price of the lot at which any participant may redeem the lot at any time. Accordingly, the auction cannot exceed the buyout price of the lot.

How do auto bids function?

Auto bids are your assistants. As a rule, auto bids are places when there are a couple of seconds left till the end of the auction. In the system of auto bids you should set such parameters as the minimum price of the goods, the maximum price of the goods and the amount of auto bids which you are planning to place. If two or more participants of the auction decided to turn auto bids on and they indicated the same run-up in prices, the bid that will function first is the bid of the person who was the first one to set up and activate auto bids. When you activate auto bids in your account the number of bids you have specified in the settings will freeze. If you cancel auto bids, all the unused bids will become available again both for manual mode and for auto bids resumption.

How is extra time reduced?

Each placed bid increments the timing device by a predetermined value. This value is specified by the administrator and can vary from 15 seconds to more time. The timing device implements the countdown.

How to place the bid

To place a bid you must have a non-zero balance.
If you have some bids - just click “place the bid"

I won the auction, what do I do?

After the auction finishes the administration checks the history of trades to avoid all the possible fraud. If the evidence of fraud or collusion between the participants is found, the auction will be cancelled and all bids will be returned to the participants, except for the bids of the winner.

After checking the history of trades, the Winner will be notified of when and where the sale of goods contract will be signed.

The winner has 10 days to clearance the goods starting from the date of winning.

Within one - three working days we will perform a final settlement for you that will include the cost of delivery to your address. Within 5 days after the confirmation of your winning you must pay the calculated amount in any way that is convenient for you.

The delivery will be made within 1-21 days

(depending on the region).

What is "Night Auction"?

This is an auction held at night from 22.00.00 to 08.59.59

What is "Training auctions"?

Training auction is a kind of auction which was specially designed for "beginners" (the members who never won in the auctions). It's a great way to gain experience and learn the general rules of the auction in detail. The member who won a real auction at least once is not considered to be a "novice".

When you sign up, we offer you an unlimited number of training bids which can only be used for participation in training auctions. Training auctions are held according to the same rules and with the same products / services as the real ones. The only difference is that the bidder doesn’t pay real money for his bids and the winning in this case is also the training one.

What is the "Day Auction"?

This is an auction held during the day from 09.00.00 to 21.59.59

What is the "Round-the-clock auction"?

This is an auction that does not depend on the time of the day
