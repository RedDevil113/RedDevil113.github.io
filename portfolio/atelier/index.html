Atelier is a members’ club for those passionate about art.

Members get access to a rotating collection of art and, in the comfort of their own homes, enjoy a new work of art every month.

APPLY NOW
DISCOVERY

You get a new art experience every month!

You get high-quality art to hang on your walls and an element of surprise every month.

LEARNING

You learn about art, history, art market, new trends, various movements and styles.

You train your eye and better understand what kind of art you would like to have in your own collection.

SUPPORT

By being a member you support emerging and up-and-coming artists.

Since the majority of works in the Club’s collection are purchased directly from the artists or from dealers representing them.

How it works

It’s very simple.
You pay a monthly membership fee and receive a new work of art every month. When a new work is delivered, the previous one is collected.

For more details, please read our Club Rules. This document covers all aspects of the Club, including insurance & security, art collection, membership, fees etc.

Additional Benefits

Atelier is proud to offer its members additional benefits. These include:

- Exclusive discounts on all Food & Drinks in the Atelier @Osprey - a unique art exhibition space and cafe in Central London (launch date to be announced soon).

- An exclusive community channel for Club Members - to engage, discuss, share your thoughts, opinions, and suggestions for new acquisitions (launch date to be announced soon).

- Exclusive Art Dinners with fun and enjoyable talks on art history, art business, art law and other topics by well-known ‘mystery guests’: artists, curators, collectors, dealers, critics and quite often celebrity speakers.
