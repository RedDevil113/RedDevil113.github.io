Here’s how you can become a member and why you’ll want to.

Joining Process
  • 1. Click the “Apply Now” button and fill in the online application form
  • 2. Once your application has been reviewed and approved, you will receive an email confirmation with a link to set up your account and direct debit.
  • 3. ATELIER’s team reserve a right to request a brief video call with you to check if you are a good fit for the Club.
  • 4. Once you have paid the joining fee and set up your direct debit, your membership will be activated. We will be in touch within 1 working days to arrange your first art delivery.
