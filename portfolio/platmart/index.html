Multiple languages and currencies

Platmart allows your webshop to speak customers language and sell your products in customers currency. You’re able to create products listings for multiple languages and add prices in multiple currencies (or use auto conversion).

Accept payments globally

To sell products worldwide you need to accept all credit and debit cards in the world. You can accept payments via Platmart with monthly/weekly withdraws to your bank account. Alternatively, you can attach your own Stripe account.
